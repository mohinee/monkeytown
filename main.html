About Monkey Town

Held just a month into the Chinese New Year of 2016 - The Year of the Monkey(coincidently, or not), at one of the untouched parts of Ooty, to give you a experience is the Monkey Town Arts & Music Festival.Monkey Town is not just another festival, it a culmination of a myriad of music and art within the nature's cradle. Get ready to give your senses a treat with the enchanting dance-forms of the local culture, fire dancers, and a whole lot of art forms, local craftwork and workshops.Escape to Monkey Town to the perfect blend of soul-stirring music, local dance forms, visual performances and fire dancing in the Queen of hills breathing in the freshest of air, the greenest of getaways, away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The festival is open to anyone above the age of 18. Not only are we music lovers but we are also environmentally conscious and the festival will be a chance for you to understand how best to preserve it.

The Classic Camp

Fall in love with the queen of hills, basking in her glory and enjoy everything that she has to offer by camping out at the venue, sleeping under the stars.We have a special package for camping, which is inclusive of the festival and breakfast for two days. Basic facilities like toilets and tents will be provided at the venue. You can come in as early by 5 pm on 25th March. So get back to the basics, enjoy your stay in pure bliss of nature and yourself.
